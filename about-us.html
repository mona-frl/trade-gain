What we are

A community created to help people offer services (and also get services) without a monetary payment but with a treat.

The idea started during the Code First Girl Web Development Course, where the participants had to create a website with a theme of their choice.

We've decided to create something different, a proposition where people could take advantage of a new trade&gain experience.

It's a great opportunity for you to start getting treated doing something you like.

Sometimes we want to explore new horizons but lack motivation, why not takes pictures or petsit whilest enjoying a treat of your choice?
Either a cup of coffe or a piece of cake, feel free to ask for anything!
